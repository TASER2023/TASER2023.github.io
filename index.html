TASER: Efficient Black-Box Attack on Speech Platforms via Perturbing Linguistic Embedding

We propose TASER, the first query-efficient black-box adversarial embedding-based attack pipeline. Based on the TASER pipeline, we propose TASER-OTL and TASER-OTA schemes to launch efficient attacks in the digital domain and in the physical playback environment on four commercial ASRs and voice assistants.
